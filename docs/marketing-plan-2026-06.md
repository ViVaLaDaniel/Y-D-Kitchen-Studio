# Y&D Kitchen Studio Marketing Plan - June 2026

## Target segments

- Spanish restaurant, cafe and catering owners in Marbella / Costa del Sol.
- Foreign owners opening or relaunching hospitality projects in Spain.
- Villa managers, concierge teams and private hospitality operators.
- Boutique hotels, retreats and premium event organizers.
- Restaurant buyers who need a practical menu and kitchen operating system after acquisition.

## Core message

Y&D does not only make dishes look good. Y&D helps owners launch a menu the kitchen can cost, prep, plate, train and repeat.

## Outreach plan

Build simple lead lists for:
- Marbella / San Pedro / Estepona restaurant owners.
- Boutique hotels and villa management companies.
- Catering and event operators.
- Real estate agents serving restaurant buyers.
- Expat business communities.
- Spanish, French, German and English-speaking hospitality groups.

First outreach message should offer a short menu profit / launch risk review, not a broad consultancy pitch.

## Advisor strategy

Use the senior Spanish advisor privately as credibility and network leverage first. Public use requires:
- Explicit consent.
- Approved name and photo.
- Verified restaurant history.
- Agreement on exact wording.

Until then, use anonymous advisory language and illustrative brand photography only.
